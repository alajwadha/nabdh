CUMULATIVE CASH OVER 12 MONTHS

Why subs can grow while cash is still negative: you pay the full CAC the moment a user joins, but they pay you back over months — so during a growth year you're always funding fresh cohorts that haven't paid back yet. Each sub is profitable on its own in ~CAC ÷ monthly-margin months; the year-1 dip is the cost of buying that recurring base.
